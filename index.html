Welcome

Congratulations, new FICSIT Pioneer! You are about to embark on a journey across the stars to a destination most fantastical: MASSAGE-2(A-B)b in the binary star system of Akycha. Once planetside, you will be tasked with finding natural resources, constructing machines to mine and process them, and automating the process to further exploit the planet. Initial surveys suggest 4 particular biomes as relatively safe landing zones.

Planetfall

Once you've decided your starting location, you'll be directed to your drop pod where an in-drop orientation will play, reminding you of your obligation to FICSIT. Once planetside ADA, your onboard virtual AI assistant will direct your first steps.

  1. Grab the build tool from the drop pod interior.
  2. Dismantle the drop pod
  3. Equip the Xeno-zapper.
  4. Locate iron ore deposits with your built in scanner.

Building the HUB

The Habitat and Utility Base or HUB is your base operations. Ammeneties include living quarters, a crafting bench, two biomass burners, the HUB Terminal, FICSIT Freighter, and a Personal Storage Box. The terminal is the point where Pioneer milestone materials are delivered.
